Mozaic Bags: Launching a D2C brand from zero in Gurugram.

Role Co-Founder
Location Gurugram, India
Focus E-Commerce · Ops · Data

Mozaic started as a concept for a direct-to-consumer bag brand. The job was to turn it into a working business — from setting up the Shopify backend and integrating LoyaltyLion, to organizing raw sales data into dashboards and managing manufacturer negotiations.

The Problem

Raw sales data scattered across spreadsheets. No unified dashboard. No structured relationship with the manufacturer. The Shopify store needed backend integration for loyalty rewards, and marketing decisions were happening without data.

"The messy part of starting up isn't the idea — it's organizing the chaos around it into something that actually runs."

What I Did

Organized sales data into clear dashboards to track performance. Managed manufacturer negotiations and timelines. Set up and maintained the Shopify backend, including LoyaltyLion integration for customer retention.

The Outcome

A functioning D2C brand with structured operations, clean data workflows, and a reliable supply chain relationship — built from scratch in Gurugram.
